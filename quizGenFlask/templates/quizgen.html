Troubleshooting & Errors
I got error "X", what should I do?

Sorry, if you contact me, I'll try to address it. Here is a short-list of known problems work-arounds, if any:

"Cannot seem to generate enough 1-20 questions to meet distribution"
This can happen, especially, when there is only 1 chapter in one of the periods. For instance, if your "past" period is chapters 1-6, and the "current" is chapter 7. There might not be enough questions drawn to fill the minimums. Often, if you rerun the tool several times, one of them will eventually work. In these cases, you should also generate quizzes one at a time to increase the likelihood of generating successful quizzes. By the times chapter ranges enlarge, this problem should go away.
The fine print and FAQ...
This is in beta. Carefully review your results before you need them!
What about the rules & distributions these were generated from?
  • This is based on the 2018 CMA rules.
  • Questions are from the CQLT "local" subset of questions.
  • A and B division questions for quote-type and finish-the-verse type questions are limited to club 150+300 and 150 respectively. The "eXtreme" division is without these limits.
The quiz document formatting doesn't look very nice...

Unfortunately, there is no control of the PDF format -- sorry! However, the Word docx format retains the intended formatting, so it may look nicer to open in Word or LibreOffice and save/print or export as PDF.
